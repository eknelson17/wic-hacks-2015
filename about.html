What is weSource?

weSource is a website designed to help students navigate to other websites that provide study sources, research materials, and games for various topics. Each link is divided not only by topic but also by grade level, so there are resources for everyone, and our simple layout makes it very easy to navigate the site!

How it Started

With the simple idea of compiling a source of educational links for students, weTech presents weSource. This website is designed to lead students to helpful websites based on their grade and the course that they select. weSource also aims at exposing students to programming: the site provides links to both a general overview of programming itself as well as a couple tutorials in basic programming languages.

Who is weSource for?

weSource is designed for those students, or those who want to deepen their knowledge. This site provides links for games, studying, and good research websites.

What is the advantage to using weSource?

There will be no more scouring the Internet for good educational websites! weSource provides links to a few quality websites that will help you in your studies.

What programs were used to create weSource?

weSource was made using HTML, Bootstrap, Sass, and SublimeText.

The weTech Team

weTech is 'Women for education and technology:' our team includes Catherine, Emma, Jordan, Kayla, Niharika, and Sarada. weTech has chosen to make this website for the purpose of streamlining resources for students so that they can more easily access educational materials.
